About Me!

Hi, I'm Alexander Humphrey, please call me Alex. My pronouns are he/him. I was born in Chicago on November 23rd, 2005, I am currently a college freshman. I'd say that usually being the youngest in my class growing up has affected me negatively and positively. I had a superiority complex as well as a sort of inferiority complex, confusing right? I always wanted to be the best in everything, I wanted to beat all my classmates in elementary and when I didn't (which was often) I'd loathe myself and beat myself up about it. Today though I've disposed of those mentalities. I don't want to be the best and I'm okay with that, I just want to be enough for myself. I'm very proud of my growth, not just with that but with so many other facets of myself. Today I feel as though I've never been more me.
I created Evergarden with aspiring artists in mind. I see artists on TikTok, Instagram, and Twitter (X?) who have masterpieces on hand and I wanted to create a centralized location for people to discover these artists and their works of art. My end goal is a thriving online community of respected artists and loyal clientele; I want these artists to play a part in the design of the website as well. For example, a community voted artist spotlight where one artist is voted to be front and center on the homepage.
