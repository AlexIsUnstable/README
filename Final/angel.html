Taylor Services Youth Organization is proud to partner with Prison Fellowship Angel Tree Program every year to help make Christmas for children whose parent is incarcerated. It is such an amazing opportunity to help be a blessing to families that need a little extra Christmas spirit. We are always here to make our families feel appreciated. A very special "THANK YOU" to all of our sponsors that have donated in the Spirit of Christmas! If you would like to sponsor a child for Christmas, please contact us at (773) 437-6592.
