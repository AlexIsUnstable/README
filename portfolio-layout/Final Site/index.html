Violence & Mental Health

Everything that exists has experienced violence, however the perception depends on perspective.

When it comes to the relationship between humanity's understanding of violence and mental health, its generally understood that violence in most forms almost always yields negative ramifications. However, this belief should be challenged. Depending on the form and expression, violence should be considered a double edges sword that reaps both positive and negative effects.
